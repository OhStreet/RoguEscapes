Game of the Week

Balatro has quickly become a titan in the roguelike genre and is already in 'top 5' talks just 8 months post-release. This deckbuilder challenges you to score points by crafting poker hands from a limited card selection. The core gameplay loop focuses on collecting and upgrading cards to build your deck while tackling a variety of encounters.

One of the standout features are the Joker cards, which add unique effects to the hands you play, allowing for creative strategies. For instance, a Joker might let you draw extra cards or modify card values, influencing your approach to each challenge.

Every encounter presents new tactical decisions about which cards to keep, which to upgrade, and how to best leverage your Joker effects. With procedural generation ensuring each playthrough feels fresh, Balatro strikes a perfect balance between casual fun and strategic depth, making it appealing for both newcomers and seasoned players alike as you explore countless ways to assemble hands and maximize your score.
